## About ChopShop

ChopShop is a complete B2C e-commerce platform designed to help vendors of all sizes sell online and deliver to their customers efficiently.

### For Vendors

Set up your online store in minutes. Manage products, track orders, and grow your business with powerful analytics.

### For Customers

Browse products, place orders, and track deliveries — all from a beautifully designed app that matches your favorite store's brand.

### For Delivery

Optimized routing, real-time tracking, and seamless handoff make deliveries efficient and transparent for everyone involved.
