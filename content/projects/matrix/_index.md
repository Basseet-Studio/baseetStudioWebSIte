## About Matrix

Matrix is the workspace that brings your team's work together. Instead of juggling separate tools for tasks, documents, and meetings, everything lives in one place.

### Why Matrix?

Teams waste hours every week switching between apps. Matrix eliminates that friction by combining the best of project management, documentation, and team communication.

### Built for Real Teams

Whether you're a startup of 3 or a company of 300, Matrix scales to fit your workflow — not the other way around.
