## About Medev

Medev is the healthcare management system built for clinics and hospitals that want full control without complexity. Whether you host it yourself or we host it for you, you get a complete solution with a customizable frontend that reflects your brand.

### Built for Modern Healthcare

Medev combines the robustness of enterprise healthcare systems with the flexibility of open-source technology. It's designed to grow with your practice — from small clinics to large hospital networks.

### Your Brand, Your Way

The included frontend website can be fully customized to match your clinic's branding and design requirements. You specify the look; we build it with the standardized Medev backend powering everything underneath.
