## About PhotoRestore AI

PhotoRestore AI combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with a passion for preserving memories. We believe every photo tells a story, and no damage should stand in the way of sharing those precious moments.

### Our Technology

Built on state-of-the-art machine learning models, PhotoRestore AI can:

- Detect and repair scratches, tears, and creases
- Restore faded or discolored areas
- Enhance image clarity and sharpness
- Colorize black & white photographs
- Remove stains and water damage

### Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. All photos are processed in isolated secure environments and automatically deleted within 24 hours of processing. Your memories stay yours.
