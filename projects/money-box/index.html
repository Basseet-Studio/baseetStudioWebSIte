Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage your finances

Smart Savings Goals

Set personalized savings goals and track your progress with beautiful visual charts. Whether saving for a vacation, emergency fund, or a new gadget, Money Box keeps you motivated.

Expense Tracking

Automatically categorize your spending and understand where your money goes. Get insights that help you make better financial decisions.

Visual Progress Charts

Beautiful charts and graphs show your savings growth over time. Celebrate milestones and stay motivated on your financial journey.

Daily Reminders

Gentle reminders help you stay on track with your savings goals. Build healthy financial habits one day at a time.

Budget Categories

Organize your expenses into custom categories. See exactly how much you spend on food, entertainment, bills, and more.

Secure & Private

Your financial data stays on your device. We use industry-standard encryption to keep your information safe and private.

About Money Box

Money Box was born from a simple idea: managing your money shouldn’t be complicated. We’ve created an intuitive app that makes saving money feel effortless and even enjoyable.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves financial peace of mind. Money Box is designed to help you build healthy money habits, one small step at a time.

Why We Built This

As developers who struggled with traditional budgeting apps, we wanted something different. Something that focuses on the positive—watching your savings grow—rather than just tracking expenses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Money Box free to use?
Money Box offers a free tier with essential features. Premium features are available through an affordable subscription.
Is my financial data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We never share your information with third parties.
When will Money Box be available?
We're currently in development and will launch soon. Sign up for early access to be the first to know!
Can I sync across devices?
Yes! Premium users can sync their data securely across all their devices using iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android).
